# Confidence — Communication Trainer

> "Help me say this naturally."

A daily practice app for non-native professionals who already speak functional English but want more natural confidence in communication.

---

## Deploy to Your Phone (Step-by-Step)

### Step 1 — Upload to GitHub

1. Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and sign in
2. Click the **+** icon (top right) → **New repository**
3. Name it: `confidence-app`
4. Set to **Public** (required for free Vercel)
5. Click **Create repository**
6. On the next screen, click **uploading an existing file**
7. Drag and drop **all the files from this folder** into the upload area
8. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 2 — Deploy on Vercel

1. Go to [vercel.com](https://vercel.com) and sign up with your GitHub account
2. Click **Add New Project**
3. Find `confidence-app` in the list → click **Import**
4. Framework preset will auto-detect as **Vite** ✓
5. Click **Deploy** — takes about 60 seconds
6. Vercel gives you a URL like: `confidence-app.vercel.app`

### Step 3 — Install on Your Phone

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow at bottom)
3. Scroll down → tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add**
5. Done — app icon appears on your home screen

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open your Vercel URL in Chrome
2. Tap the **three dots** menu (top right)
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Add**

## What's Inside

- **Phrase Practice** — Real situations, write your version, see tone ladder + natural alternative
- **Vocabulary in Context** — Fill-in-the-blank, unlock word meaning + tone feel + real examples
- **Review Queue** — Spaced repetition: 1 day → 3 days → 7 days → 14 days

---

## Tech Stack

- React 18
- Vite
- vite-plugin-pwa (makes it installable as a phone app)
- No backend, no database — runs entirely in the browser
